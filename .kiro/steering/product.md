# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Bickqr is an SEO-first library of short audio clips ("bicks") that enables users to search trending sounds, play them instantly, and share rich links. The platform processes uploads into optimized CDN assets, waveform cover images, and teaser MP4 previews for enhanced social media embeds.

## Target Users
- **Everyday social media users**: Quick discovery and sharing of trending sounds
- **Content creators**: Finding, attributing, and using sounds in their content
- **Bick uploaders**: Creators who upload and manage their own audio clips

## Key Features
- SEO-optimized sound library with search and discovery
- Instant audio playback with waveform visualization
- Rich link sharing with preview embeds (OG images, MP4 teasers)
- User upload system with async processing pipeline
- CDN-delivered media for fast global access
- Trending sounds discovery and attribution

## Business Objectives
- Build a comprehensive, searchable library of short audio clips
- Maximize social sharing through rich embeds and previews
- Enable creators to upload and manage their content
- Achieve strong SEO rankings for sound-related searches

## User Journey
1. **Discovery**: User searches or browses trending bicks
2. **Preview**: Instant playback with waveform visualization
3. **Share**: Copy rich link with auto-generated preview assets
4. **Upload** (creators): Submit audio → async processing → published bick

## Success Criteria
- Fast search and playback (<100ms perceived latency)
- High-quality social embeds that drive engagement
- Reliable upload processing pipeline
- Strong organic search traffic
